📈 MSP Growth Over Time

Key Takeaways

  • Ragi saw the highest MSP increase (186%) over the 12‑year period.
  • All crops have received consistent hikes, with a sharp jump in 2018‑19 due to the government’s policy of fixing MSP at 1.5x production cost.
  • Pulses (Arhar, Moong) show absolute MSP values far above cereals, reflecting their nutritional importance.
